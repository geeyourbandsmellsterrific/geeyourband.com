Celebrate Valentine’s Day with Gee, Your Band Smells Terrific as they return to Trillions Sports Bar for their second appearance. If you’re looking for a fun, upbeat way to dance together and keep the night going, this return performance is the perfect Valentine’s outing with a retro twist.
