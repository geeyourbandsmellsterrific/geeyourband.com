Party In The Park is back, and we’re bringing the Boogie!Join Gee, Your Band Smells Terrific for a FREE summer evening of disco, funk, yacht rock, and singalong favorites at Chamberlin Park beginning at 7pm. Bring your lawn chairs, your dancing shoes, and your favorite people. We’ll bring the super hits of the ’70s and a whole lot of Good Times. Peace, Love & Music. See you in Deer Park!



**Details**

* Parking is available from the Blue Ash Road or Plainfield Road entrances. Handicap parking is available with placards in the city lot and Community Center lot.
* There are a few picnic tables, but chairs and blankets are encouraged. Bring the kids, bring your neighbors, and bring your pups too (on a leash, please)!
* The Deer Park Park Board will have concessions available while supplies last, including hamburgers, brats, metts, hot dogs, popcorn, candy, soda, water, beer and White Claw.
* No outside alcohol permitted. Please leave coolers at home.
* Food and beverage purchases now accept Tap & Go payment methods. Cash is still required for Split the Pot and the Summer Raffle.

Drawing held September 19, 2026.
